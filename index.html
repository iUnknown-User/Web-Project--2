Treaty 1

Treaty 1 was negotiated at Lower Fort Garry in Manitoba.

Which communities were involved in the signing of tready 1?

These were the communities that were involved in forming Treaty One: the Anishinaabe, Muskegon Cree peoples, and the Crown.

About the communities

The Anishinaabe originated on the northeast coast of what is now Canada and the United States.

The Muskegon were native-born Americans from New England, New York, and Pennsylvania.

The Crown refers to the royals from England.

What were the terms to the aggreement to tready 1?

The terms discussed in the agreement of Treaty One said that "In Treaty One, the band of Yellow Quill will resive a further 25 square miles of land to be laid out around the reserve."

The indiginous peoples were given 160 acres of land per family

Also in the agreement, the Indigenous peoples were to hand over a large portion of land in the southeast and south-central current-day Manitoba to Governor Archibald and the Crown.
